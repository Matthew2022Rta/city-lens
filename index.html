Know your rights

What a Notice to Cure really means.

①
It is not an eviction. No one can remove you from your home without a court case and a judge's order. A notice is the very beginning of a process.
②
You have the right to "cure." This notice gives you 10 days to fix the issue the landlord claims. If you fix it, the matter can end there.
③
You have the right to a lawyer. In NYC, many tenants qualify for a free lawyer in housing court through the Right to Counsel law.
④
Your status doesn't matter here. Tenant protections apply to everyone, regardless of immigration status. You cannot be reported for using these rights.
⑤
Keep everything. Save the notice, envelopes, texts, and receipts. Photograph them. They protect you later.
